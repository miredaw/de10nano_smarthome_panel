FPGA Alarm Logic (alarm_logic_0 @ 0xFF200060)

Bit 0 — ALARM_TEMP_HIGH — Temperature > 35°C
Bit 1 — ALARM_TEMP_LOW — Temperature < 15°C
Bit 2 — ALARM_LIGHT_LOW — Light < 100 ADC counts
Bit 3 — ALARM_MOTION — PIR1 or PIR2 active (0xFF200040)
Bit 4 — ALARM_CRITICAL — Composite critical flag
Bitmask 0x18 — Motion + Critical active simultaneously
