ℹ️ System Information

Board:Terasic DE10-Nano
HPS Processor:ARM Cortex-A9 Dual-Core @ 800 MHz
FPGA:Intel Cyclone V SE 5CSEBA6U23I7
Memory:1 GB DDR3 SDRAM
BME280 Module:Waveshare Environmental Sensor — I²C 0x76, ADDR→GND, built-in 4.7kΩ pull-ups
BME280 Pins:SDA=JP1 Pin 1 (PIN_V12), SCL=JP1 Pin 2 (PIN_E8), VCC=JP1 Pin 30 (3.3V), GND=JP1 Pin 29
MCP3008 ADC:10-bit SPI — CLK=JP1 Pin 3 (PIN_W12), MOSI=Pin 4 (D11), MISO=Pin 5 (D8), CS=Pin 6 (AH13)
ADC Channels:CH0=Light (photoresistor), CH1=Heating, CH2=Sound
PIR Sensors:2× HC-SR501 — PIR1=JP1 Pin 7 (PIN_AF7), PIR2=JP1 Pin 8 (PIN_AH14)
ESP32-WROOM-32:HPS UART1 (/dev/ttyS1), 115200 bps — TX=JP1 Pin 21 (PIN_C12), RX=JP1 Pin 22 (PIN_AD17)
SIM800L EVB:Altera UART MMIO 0xFF200080, 9600 bps — TX=JP1 Pin 19 (PIN_D12), RX=JP1 Pin 20 (PIN_AD20)
SIM800L Power:5V from external DC-DC buck (≥2A); EVB onboard LDO → 4V for SIM800L chip
LW Bridge Base:0xFF200000
